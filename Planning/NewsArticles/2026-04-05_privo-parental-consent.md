# TrashMob Partners with PRIVO to Protect Young Volunteers

## Excerpt

TrashMob.eco is proud to announce our partnership with PRIVO, a leading children's privacy technology company, to bring COPPA-compliant parental consent and identity verification to our platform. This makes TrashMob one of the safest places for families to volunteer together.

## Body

### Why Youth Volunteer Safety Matters

Community cleanup events are a powerful way for families to teach environmental responsibility. But when minors participate in online platforms, their safety and privacy must come first. That's why we've partnered with PRIVO to build industry-leading protections for our youngest volunteers.

### What is PRIVO?

PRIVO is a leading children's privacy technology company that provides FTC-approved identity verification and parental consent services. They help organizations comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) while creating safe digital experiences for kids and families.

TrashMob is the first organization to integrate Microsoft Entra External ID with PRIVO — a groundbreaking approach that combines modern identity management with children's privacy compliance.

### How It Works

**For Parents:**
1. **Verify your identity** — A one-time verification through PRIVO's secure widget confirms you are who you say you are.
2. **Add your child** — Enter your child's information on your dashboard and approve which features they can access.
3. **Sign waivers** — When your child wants to attend an event, you'll receive an email to sign the required waivers on their behalf.

**For Young Volunteers (13-17):**
1. **Create an account** — After your parent approves, you'll receive an invitation to create your own TrashMob account.
2. **Find and attend events** — Browse events, register, and make a difference in your community.
3. **Track your impact** — See your contributions on leaderboards and earn achievements.

### Built-In Protections

We've implemented comprehensive protections for minor volunteers:

- **Name privacy** — Only first name and last initial are shown publicly
- **No direct messaging** — Minors cannot send or receive private messages
- **Adult presence required** — At least one adult must be registered for any event with minor participants
- **Parent-controlled features** — Parents decide which platform features their child can access through PRIVO
- **Waiver routing** — All waivers must be signed by a parent, never by the minor

### Feature-Level Parental Controls

Through PRIVO, parents can individually approve or restrict access to:
- Event registration and attendance
- Public leaderboard visibility
- Social media sharing
- Newsletter subscriptions
- GPS route tracking
- Team membership
- Photo uploads

### A First-of-Its-Kind Integration

TrashMob is proud to be the first platform to combine Microsoft Entra External ID (our identity provider) with PRIVO's consent management system. This integration is being documented as a reference implementation for other organizations looking to build safe youth experiences on modern identity platforms.

### Get Started

If you're a parent interested in getting your child involved in community cleanups:

1. Create your free TrashMob account at [trashmob.eco](https://www.trashmob.eco)
2. Verify your identity on your dashboard
3. Add your child as a dependent
4. Start volunteering together!

For questions about our parental consent process, visit our [FAQ page](https://www.trashmob.eco/faq) or [contact us](https://www.trashmob.eco/contactus).
