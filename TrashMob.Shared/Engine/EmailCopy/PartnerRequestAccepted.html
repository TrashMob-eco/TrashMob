Congratulations! Your request to become a TrashMob.eco Partner has been accepted! Thank you so much for joining the team!

What's Next?

  1. The first step is to log in to the TrashMob.eco account that created this request, and go to your Dashboard. Look for the section titled "My Partnerships"
  2. Select the ... in the Action Column next to you partnership, and click 'Activate Partnership'. Note that Partnerships are created in an inactive state until you are ready to activate them.
  3. Make sure the name and website for your partnership is correct.
  4. Add / Update your Partner Contacts as needed. Save changes.
  5. Add / Update your Partner Admins as needed. Save changes.
  6. Add / Update your Partner Social Media Accounts as needed. Save changes.
  7. Add / Update your Partner Documents as needed. Save changes.
  8. Add / Update your Partner Locations as needed
    • Set up the details of the location by filling out the form and setting the physical location by clicking on the map. Save changes.
    • Add / Update your Partner Location Contacts as needed. You need to have at least one contact per partner location in order for events to use your services. Save changes.
    • Add / Update your Partner Location Services as needed. You need to have at least one service offering per partner location in order for events to use your services. Save changes.
  9. When all looks good, activate the partner and the partner location and save your changes.

Leads for events in your area are always looking for help in their cleanups. They will send a request for assistance through TrashMob.eco, and you will be notified when that happens. When you receive that email, please sign back in to TrashMob.eco, and accept their request through the My Dashboard page.

I don't see any events near me. Can I create an event?

Absolutely! Create an event and add yourself as a partner, then use social media or whatever you can do to get people in your community out to help with an event. We do ask that you actually participate in the cleanup as well, just to make sure it actually goes off without a hitch.

Need help?

Please email us! We want your Partnership to be a great experience for you and your community. Let us know if you run into any issues or have suggestions for improvements to our site or our programs!
