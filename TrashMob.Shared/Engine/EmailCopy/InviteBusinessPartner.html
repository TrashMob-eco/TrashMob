TrashMob.eco is a non-profit organization that helps communities organize and schedule litter cleanups. The service is completely free for both communities and citizens. That you are getting this email means that someone in your community has joined TrashMob.eco, and would like your organization to become a TrashMob.eco Partner!

What does being a TrashMob.eco Partner mean?

There are a number of logistical challenges that TrashMob.eco Partners help the volunteers to overcome:

  • Where do they get starter kits? (buckets, litter pickers, gloves, safety vests)
  • Where do they get safety gear and supplies? (signs, bags)
  • How do they get the picked litter from the cleanup location to the disposal site? Many volunteers don't have vehicles capable of hauling more than one or two bags of litter and often community picks can result in a dozen or more bags. Sometimes these picking locations are in areas where only vehicles with safety lights can stop. If you have trucks with safety lights and have a few minutes a week to help, that can go a long way to getting litter out of the community!
  • How do they dispose of the litter? Most household garbage cans are small and might fit one bag of garbage, but as a local business, you may have a dumpster with excess capacity at the end of the week. Being a partner could be as simple as allowing TrashMob.eco to drop off a few bags of picked garbage in your dumpster a couple of times a month.
  • How do they raise awareness of their efforts to clean up the community in order to attract more volunteers? Business Partners can help with social media and advertising to help build the number of volunteers active in the community.

TrashMob.eco provides a way for partners to declare which services you want to provide the volunteers, and automates the process by which users can request these services.

Why should my business become a TrashMob.eco Partner?

Keeping your community clean supports the local economy. Businesses in clean communities see increased traffic. Businesses who actively support community cleanups earn goodwill in the community and become seen as community leaders in the fight against pollution, and help to protect water quality and wildlife in the area.

So how do I get started as a TrashMob.eco Partner?

It takes just a few minutes of your time to get started:

  1. Create an Account on TrashMob.eco
  2. Fill out and submit the Become a Partner form
  3. TrashMob.eco staff will process your application and send you a response with instructions on how to move forward!
